About Me

Hi, I'm Sanjana Ganesh — a developer, designer, and someone who genuinely loves building calm, meaningful digital experiences. I’m drawn to soft aesthetics, clean layouts, and thoughtful technology that feels good to use.

My journey is a blend of creativity and logic. I enjoy taking an idea, shaping its visual personality, and then turning it into a real, functional product. Whether it’s an app, a website, or a tiny interface detail — I always start with emotion and clarity. How should it feel? How should it guide the user? How can I make it gentle, intuitive, and beautiful?

I specialize in Flutter, front-end development, and UI/UX design, with a strong interest in user-centric systems. My biggest project, Pawsify, grew from my love for dogs and my wish to use tech to support pet owners, fosters, and rescue communities. It taught me product thinking, teamwork, architecture, and how to shape a digital experience from scratch.

Right now, I’m focusing on improving my development craft, building a strong portfolio, and finding an internship opportunity for January 2026 where I can grow, contribute, and work with a team that values detail, empathy, and design-led development.
